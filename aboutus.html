About Us

Project Description

The selection of a specialization within the computer science field can be difficult for students who are unsure of their future path within the software field. This website provides a comprehensive set of resources meant to help students discover their potential specialization. It includes resources to inform students of the differences between specializations, as well as a specialization matcher that matches preferred classes to specializations that those classes fulfill.
